Well, I will not discuss here about the techniques about the literature review, but more on the tools I use to manage to my academic writing. I will list the tools and how I use them to achieve better efficiency in academic writing. Let's dive in. 1. Sioyek: Most of you might not have heard about it, but this PDF reader saves a lot of your time. Some of you might own iPad or tablets, where you can easily annotate; in such case, you might not need this, but for those who mainly use computer or are mostly comfortable, sioyek can be your best PDF reader.
